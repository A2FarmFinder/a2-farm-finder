## Ready to Add

### Chelsea Community Fair
- **Date:** Monday, August 25 – Saturday, August 29, 2026
- **Time:** Varies by day — see fair schedule for daily hours
- **Location:** Chelsea Community Fairgrounds
- **Address:** 20501 Old US-12 Hwy, Chelsea, MI 48118
- **Admission:** $10/day; $30 season pass; $20 student/senior/veteran pass. Free for children 10 and under, and free during Senior Day (Thu before 5pm), Ladies' Day (Fri before 11am), Military Day (Fri), and Family Day (Fri 11am–3pm)

- **Source:** [chelseafair.org](https://www.chelseafair.org/), [Admissions & Passes](https://www.chelseafair.org/admissions---passes)
- **Notes:** Not currently listed in `/src/content/events/`. Good fit for "Festival & Fair" category, same as Saline Community Fair.

### Friday Family Fun Night at Robin Hills Farm
- **Date:** Recurring Fridays through summer 2026 (next occurrence: Friday, July 17, 2026; also July 24, 31, and continuing on a weekly basis)
- **Time:** 5:00pm – 10:00pm
- **Location:** Robin Hills Farm — "The Nest" Bar + Patio
- **Address:** 20390 M-52, Chelsea, MI 48118
- **Admission:** Free
- **Description:** Weekly family evening at Robin Hills Farm (already listed on the site) with children's activities and scavenger hunts on the farm trails, food and bonfire s'mores. Open to the general public.
- **Source:** [Robin Hills Farm Events page](https://www.robinhillsfarm.com/robin-hills-farm-events)
- **Notes:** Robin Hills Farm ([robin-hills-farm.md](../src/content/farms/robin-hills-farm.md)) is already a listed farm — flagging per the farm cross-reference check. This is one of several recurring weekly offerings at the farm (there are also ticketed adult-oriented "Patio Night" music/bar events and paid brunches on other days) — only the free, family-oriented Friday night is included here as it best matches the site's family/kids farm activity focus.
