The 4th of July weekend is one of the best of the entire summer in Washtenaw County — and this year there's plenty to do, eat, and celebrate locally. Here's your complete guide to making the most of it.

## Events This Weekend

### Ann Arbor Jaycees 4th of July Parade
**Saturday, July 4 | 10am | Downtown Ann Arbor**

The 36th Annual Ann Arbor Jaycees parade rolls through downtown on the 4th. Free to attend — stake out your spot on State Street early. Bring a blanket and enjoy the show before heading to a market or farm stand.

### 96th Annual Ypsilanti 4th of July Parade
**Saturday, July 4 | 11am–12:30pm | Ypsilanti Water Tower to Depot Town**

Ypsi's parade is one of the longest-running in the region — 96 years and counting. The route goes from the historic Water Tower down to Depot Town, where you can grab lunch afterward at one of the local spots.

## Farmers Markets Open This Weekend

Most of the regular Saturday markets are running as usual — here's where to shop:

- **Ann Arbor Farmers Market** — Saturday, 7am–3pm, 315 Detroit St
- **Saline Farmers Market** — Saturday, 8am–1pm, downtown Saline
- **Chelsea Farmers Market** — Saturday, 8am–1pm, downtown Chelsea
- **Ypsilanti Farmers Market** — Saturday, 9am–2pm, Depot Town

**What to look for this weekend:** Zucchini, green beans, cucumbers, kale, herbs, scallions, and the very first early tomatoes. Blueberries are just beginning to arrive at some vendors — ask around when you get there. Farm eggs, fresh bread, and local honey are always in good supply.

**Pro tip:** Go early. Saturday before a holiday weekend is one of the busiest market days of the year. Vendors often sell out of their best items by 9–10am.

## Farm Stands & U-Pick This Weekend

Several local farms have stands and u-pick operations open through the holiday weekend:

**Jacob's Fresh Farm** (475 N Fletcher Rd, Dexter) — Open daily 8am–6pm. Farmstand with fresh produce, raw honey, and flowers. This is the farm hosting the [Lavender Bloom Festival](/events/lavender-bloom-festival-2026) on July 18–19 — if you stop by this weekend, you'll see the lavender fields in full bloom leading up to the festival.

**Kapnick Orchards** (9903 Sayers Rd, Britton) — Open for the season with blueberries, raspberries, and more. Check their website for weekend hours and availability.

**Wasem Fruit Farm** (3898 Judd Rd, Milan) — Open for the season with fresh-picked produce and their farm market.

- **Zucchini and summer squash** — perfect on the grill
- **Cucumbers and herbs** — great for a quick salad (try our [Quick Cucumber Dill Salad](/recipes/quick-cucumber-dill-salad))
