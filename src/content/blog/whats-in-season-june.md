---
title: "What's in Season in June Around Ann Arbor"
description: "A look at what local farms are harvesting in June and where to find it at area farmers markets."
pubDate: 2026-06-01
---

June is the start of the real harvest season in southeast Michigan. Here's
what to expect at local markets and farm stands:

- **Strawberries** — peak season, often available for U-pick at area farms
- **Asparagus** — tail end of the season, grab it while you can
- **Salad greens** — lettuce, spinach, and arugula are abundant
- **Early greens** — kale, chard, and collards
- **Herbs** — basil, dill, and cilantro starting to come in

Check the [Ann Arbor Farmers Market](/markets) on Wednesdays or Saturdays
for the widest selection — it's the only market in the area open twice a
week through June — or look up a [CSA](/farms) like Tantre Farm or Blue
Spring Farm if you want this delivered weekly without the trip. U-pick
cherries also start at [Wasem Fruit Farm](/farms/wasem-fruit-farm) toward the
end of the month.
