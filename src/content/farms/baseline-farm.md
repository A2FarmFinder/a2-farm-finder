Baseline Farm raises 100% grass-fed heritage cattle on roughly 200 acres of
rotationally-grazed pasture near Dexter. No hormones, antibiotics, or
vaccines are used. Beef is sold in whole, half, and quarter-animal shares,
with some retail availability through local partners.
