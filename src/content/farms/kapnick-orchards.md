Kapnick Orchards runs a year-round farm market a few miles south of Tecumseh.
They also offer a 17-week summer CSA (June 9–September 29, 2026) with fruit
and vegetable shares available at their store and multiple farmers markets
across southeast Michigan.

**2026 CSA Pricing:**
- **Large Share** — Early pay (by Apr 6): $600 | After Apr 7: $650
- **Small Share** — Early pay (by Apr 6): $325 | After Apr 7: $350
- **Single Share** — Early pay (by Apr 6): $175 | After Apr 7: $185

**Pickup Options (Tuesdays start each week):**
- Store pickup: Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday, Friday, or Sunday
- Ann Arbor Market: Wed & Sat 7am–2pm
- Chelsea Market: Wed 10am–3pm, Sat 8am–1pm
- Northville Market: Thu 8am–2pm
- Pittsfield Market: Thu 2–6pm
- Saline Market: Sat 8am–12pm
- Farmington Market: Sat 9am–2pm

Payment by cash, check (payable to Kapnick Orchards), or credit/debit card.
