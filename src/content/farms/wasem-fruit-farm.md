Wasem Fruit Farm is a family-run u-pick orchard just south of Washtenaw
County between Ypsilanti and Milan. U-pick apples (a dozen+ varieties) run
from shortly after Labor Day through early November, with tart cherries in
late June/July and pumpkins in October. Their cider mill and donuts are a
fall favorite, and they also sell at the Ann Arbor Farmers Market.
