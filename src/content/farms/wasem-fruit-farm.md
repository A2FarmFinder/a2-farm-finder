Wasem Fruit Farm is a family-run u-pick orchard just south of Washtenaw
County. They are currently open **Fridays only, 9am-5pm through December 19th**.
Offerings include u-pick apples and raspberries, fresh cider, donuts, local
honey, and homemade jams. Cash is preferred but cards are accepted. Check
their Facebook page for the most up-to-date hours and apple picking schedule
before visiting.
