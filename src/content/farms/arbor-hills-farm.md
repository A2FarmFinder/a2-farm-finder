Arbor Hills Farm is a small organic farm located at the Tilian Farm
Development Center on Pontiac Trail in Ann Arbor. Founded in 2014 by Katie
Shafer, the farm grows heirloom and locally-adapted vegetable varieties in a
biodynamic setting and raises chickens.

CSA shares are available to community members and local restaurants, with
fresh produce delivered on a regular basis. Contact the farm through their
Facebook page for current CSA pricing, availability, and pickup details.
