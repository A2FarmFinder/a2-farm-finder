Robin Hills Farm is an agriculture, education, and event center nestled in
the rolling hills of Sylvan Township just outside Chelsea. Founded in 2014,
the farm grows fresh produce, florals, mushrooms in their forest area, and
raises trout in stocked ponds. They also partner with the University of
Michigan on aquaponics systems.

The farm previously ran a CSA program for two and a half years and has
expressed plans to relaunch it — "we hope fresh veggies + fish will be
coming back soon." Currently partnering with Hay Creek Heritage Farms for
produce. Check their website or contact them directly for the latest CSA
updates.

In addition to agriculture, Robin Hills hosts private events, educational
programs, and public gatherings. **The Nest Bar + Patio Beer Garden** is
open to the public Friday and Saturday, 5–10pm.
