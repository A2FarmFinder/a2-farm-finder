Argus Farm Stop isn't a single farm but a local hub: farmers set their own
prices and keep 70% of sales, with Argus operating as a farm-stand/grocery
hybrid across multiple Ann Arbor locations. Their weekly produce box ($5
delivery to Ann Arbor/Ypsilanti) and monthly meat subscription are an easy
way to get a mix of local farms' goods without picking a single CSA.
