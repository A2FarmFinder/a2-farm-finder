Marilla Field & Flora is a sustainable specialty cut flower farm near Dexter offering weekly fresh flower CSA subscriptions and custom floral design services. The farm specializes in locally grown, chemical-free seasonal blooms.

## Flower CSA Options

- **Peony CSA:** $150 — 2-3 weeks of peony bouquets at peak spring bloom
- **Spring CSA:** $145 — weekly bouquets through spring (April–May)
- **Full Season CSA:** $565 — weekly bouquets from May through October

Shares include a mix of seasonal specialty flowers harvested fresh each week.

## Floral Design

Marilla Field & Flora also offers custom floral design for weddings, events, and special occasions using sustainably grown, locally sourced flowers.

## Farm Practices

No synthetic pesticides, herbicides, or fertilizers. Flowers are grown using sustainable practices that support soil health and pollinators.
