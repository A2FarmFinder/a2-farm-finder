Fusilier Family Farms is a multi-generational family operation in Manchester
growing top-quality produce and flowers. Their products are available through
their on-farm stand, local farmers markets, and a home delivery service —
making it one of the more convenient options for getting fresh local produce
in the western Washtenaw County area.

CSA shares are available. Contact them for current season pricing and
availability.
