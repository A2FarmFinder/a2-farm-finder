Backbeat Flower Farm is a specialty cut flower farm on Kalmbach Road near Chelsea, growing a wide variety of seasonal blooms from spring through fall. Their roadside farm stand opens Friday and Saturday afternoons from May through October.

## Farm Stand

- **Hours:** Fridays & Saturdays, 10:00am – dusk
- **Season:** May – October
- **Location:** On-farm at 4885 Kalmbach Rd, Chelsea (self-serve honor stand)

## What to Expect

Freshly cut seasonal flowers including peonies, sunflowers, zinnias, dahlias, lisianthus, and more — all grown without synthetic pesticides.

## No CSA

Backbeat Flower Farm does not currently offer a flower CSA subscription. Visit the farm stand or follow their social media for availability updates.
