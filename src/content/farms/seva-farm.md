Seva Farms is a community-supported neighborhood farm leasing pesticide-free
land in Scio Township near Dexter. Lead farmer Mohinder Singh brings over two
decades of organic and biodynamic gardening expertise to the operation, which
uses no-till, regenerative methods and biodynamic preparations to produce
highly nutritious seasonal vegetables and flowers.

CSA options for the season:
- **Summer CSA Full Share** — $495 for 12 weeks
- **Summer CSA Half Share** — $245 for 12 weeks
- **Fall CSA Full Share** — $495 for 10 weeks
- **Fall CSA Half Share** — $245 for 10 weeks
- **Summer Flower Share** — $165

Gift certificates are also available starting at $40. Sign up through their
website.
