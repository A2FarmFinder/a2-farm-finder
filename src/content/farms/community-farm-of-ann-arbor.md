Community Farm of Ann Arbor was Michigan's very first CSA, founded in 1988,
and continues to operate as a member-run cooperative practicing biodynamic
agriculture. CSA shares typically range from about $550-$950/year depending
on size, with an emphasis on community involvement and on-farm education.
