Raindance Organic Farm is a woman-owned, family-run flower farm cultivating
blooms on 3 acres of their 44-acre property in Whitmore Lake, just north of
Ann Arbor. They practice certified organic, pollinator-friendly growing with
solar power, permanent cover crops, and fair wages.

Fresh flower bouquets and bulk "Buckets of Blooms" (perfect for DIY
arrangements) are available year-round at:
- **Argus Farm Stop** — Ann Arbor
- **Harvest Market** — Ann Arbor
- **Agricole Farm Stop** — Chelsea

**U-Pick Flower Events** run July through September at the farm — check their
website and Instagram (@RaindanceFarmFlowers) for event dates and ticket info.

**Bouquet Subscriptions** are available through GoFarmhand, serving much of
Washtenaw County.
