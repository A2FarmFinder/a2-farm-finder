Hand Sown Farm is a certified organic 10-acre operation in Manchester, run
by Megan and Andrew DeLeeuw out of an 1839 farmhouse. The farm uses multiple
hoop houses and passive solar infrastructure to extend the growing season,
specializing in salad mix, heirloom tomatoes, carrots, kale, chard, and
wildflower arrangements.

Their produce is available through a CSA program and at Busch's grocery
stores. The farm also welcomes apprentices and volunteers — contact them
directly for information on their CSA, current pricing, and availability.
