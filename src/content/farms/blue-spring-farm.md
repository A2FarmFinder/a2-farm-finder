Blue Spring Farm is a small family farm about 5 miles from downtown Ann
Arbor, growing organically for 17+ years. Their summer CSA runs 18 weeks
(June-September) for around $650, with a choice of Wednesday or Friday
pickup, plus optional weekly egg or flower add-ons. They also sell at Argus
Farm Stop and their own roadside stand.
