Blue Spring Farm is a small family farm about 5 miles from downtown Ann
Arbor, growing organically for 17+ years. They offer one of the most flexible
CSA structures in the area with five share options:

- **Early Season** — 7 weeks (mid-May through late June): $236–$304
- **Mid Season** — 7 weeks (early July through late August): $256–$384
- **Late Season** — 7 weeks (early September through mid-October): $288–$432
- **Full Season** — 21 weeks (mid-May through mid-October): $720–$1,080
- **Half Season** — 10 bi-weekly pickups (mid-May through early October): $360–$540

Pricing uses a sliding scale — pay 20% above, at, or 20% below market value
based on your financial circumstances. Registration for 2026 is currently
closed; check their website for 2027 sign-up dates.

They also sell at Argus Farm Stop and their own roadside stand.
