Tantre Farm has been growing ecologically since 1993 (formerly certified
organic for nearly 20 years, now practicing the same standards independently)
and offers one of the area's most established CSA programs: an 18-week summer
share (June through September) for around $650, with pickup locations in both
Chelsea and Ann Arbor. The 115-acre farm also features a HoneyBee U-Pick,
eggs, and sells at the Ann Arbor and Chelsea Farmers Markets.
