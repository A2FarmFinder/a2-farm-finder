Tantre Farm has been growing ecologically since 1993 (formerly certified
organic for nearly 20 years, now practicing the same standards independently)
and offers one of the area's most established CSA programs.

**2026 Summer CSA:**
- **Whole Season** — $685 for 18 weeks (late May through end of September)
- Serves 2 people generously or a family of 4 with small children
- **Split Share** — split a full share with another member, each pays half ($342.50). The farm can help match you with a share partner if needed.
- Sign up by **May 23** for the whole season; after that date shares are prorated
- Full payment due before the season starts

Pickup locations in both Chelsea and Ann Arbor. The 115-acre farm also
features a HoneyBee U-Pick, eggs, and sells at the Ann Arbor and Chelsea
Farmers Markets.
