Jacob's Fresh Farm spans 40 acres just 15 minutes from downtown Ann Arbor in
Dexter. Half the land is devoted to produce for their farm markets and CSA
program; ten acres are u-pick crops including strawberries, lavender,
sunflowers, and pumpkins. The remaining land serves as pollinator habitat
with wetlands and an apiary producing hundreds of gallons of raw honey
each year.

CSA shares are available across five seasons — Spring, Summer, Fall,
Thanksgiving, and Holiday — making it one of the most flexible CSA programs
in Washtenaw County. Their year-round farmstand is open daily 8am-6pm.
You can also find Jacob's produce at the Chelsea Farmers Market (Saturdays
8am-1pm, Wednesdays 1-5pm) and at Argus Farm Stop in Ann Arbor.
