Brines Farm is a regenerative and agroforestry operation in Whitmore Lake, just north of Ann Arbor. The farm practices no-till growing and integrates trees, perennials, and annual vegetables to build long-term soil health.

## CSA Shares

- **Summer CSA:** June – October
- **Winter CSA:** November – March
- Contact the farm for current pricing and availability

## Where to Find Them

- Ann Arbor Farmers Market (Saturday mornings)
- Direct from the farm

## Farm Philosophy

Brines Farm focuses on ecological farming methods including cover cropping, composting, and agroforestry — growing food while actively restoring the land.
