Green Things Farm Collective (GTFC) is a majority women-owned organic farm
LLC formed in 2020 from the merger of Ann Arbor Seed Company, the Land Loom,
and Green Things Farm. Operating on 100+ acres at 3825 Nixon Rd in Ann Arbor,
the collective practices minimal tillage and regenerative agriculture to grow
organic produce, flowers, spring seedlings, and beef.

The farm stand is open March through early December, 7 days a week (hours
vary; credit cards only). You can also find their produce at the Ann Arbor
Farmers Market on Wednesdays and Saturdays (May-December) and at Argus Farm
Stop year-round.

Membership options for 2026 include a Salad Club Punch Card and Supporter
Share — check their website for current pricing and availability.
