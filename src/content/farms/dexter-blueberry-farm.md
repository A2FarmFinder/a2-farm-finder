Dexter Blueberry Farm is a u-pick only blueberry operation right in Dexter, making it one of the closest dedicated blueberry farms to Ann Arbor. Blueberries are the only crop — no produce stands, no pre-picked berries, just a working blueberry field open to the public during the season.

**Season:** Blueberry season typically begins in July and runs approximately 4–6 weeks, depending on growing conditions each year.

**Price:** $2.75 per pound. Cash (exact change preferred) and credit/debit cards accepted.

**How it works:** The farm rotates between two field locations at 11024 Beach Rd and 10804 Beach Rd. Follow the signs on arrival to find the current picking area.

**What to bring:** A clean bucket (or borrow one from the farm), and dress for an outdoor setting — the fields are a natural bog environment.

**Rules:** Children must be supervised at all times. No pets in the crop areas (certified service dogs excepted). No smoking or alcohol on the property.

**Growing methods:** Conventional (not organic).

**Call ahead:** Hours are not posted on their website and the season start date varies year to year. Always call 734-426-2900 before making the drive to confirm they're open and picking conditions are good.
