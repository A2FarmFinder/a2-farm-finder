Country Valley Farm is a USDA-certified organic vegetable farm located on the northeastern edge of Ann Arbor. The farm delivers fresh, organic weekly boxes directly to homes across more than 50 southeast Michigan communities — no pickup required.

## CSA Box Options

| Size | Price/Week | Best For |
|------|-----------|----------|
| Small | $38 | 1–2 people |
| Medium | ~$58 | 2–3 people |
| Large | ~$88 | 3–4 people |
| Friends | $128 | Large households |

- **Term options:** 10-week or 20-week
- **Delivery:** Home delivery to 50+ SE Michigan communities including Ann Arbor, Ypsilanti, Saline, and beyond
