Dog Star Farm is a certified organic operation at 4700 Whitmore Lake Rd in Ann Arbor, started in 2025 by Magda and Zach — both experienced farm apprentices who trained at farms across the US before launching their own. Their approach: soil-building, affordable, community-centered agriculture.

## Flex CSA

Dog Star's CSA program offers more flexibility than a traditional share — members choose exactly what goes in their box each week rather than receiving a pre-packed assortment.

- **Cost:** $325 for 12 weeks (~$27/week)
- **Season:** June 27 – September 12, 2026
- **How it works:** Select your items online before noon Thursday; shares are packed Friday for Saturday pickup. If you don't choose, a "farmer's choice" selection is packed automatically.
- **Pickup locations:** Two locations (check website for current pickup spots)
- **Member perks:** 15% discount on produce, exclusive bulk deals, weekly recipe emails, and an invitation to the end-of-season potluck

**2026 CSA is sold out.** Check [dogstarfarmmi.com](https://www.dogstarfarmmi.com/flex-csa) in early 2027 to sign up for next season.

## Where to Find Their Produce

**On-farm:** Self-serve farm stand at 4700 Whitmore Lake Rd, Ann Arbor

**Farmers market:** Ypsilanti Farmers Market — every Saturday, 9am–1pm, 16 S Washington St, Ypsilanti

**Retail:** Both [Argus Farm Stop](https://www.argusfarmstop.com/) locations in Ann Arbor (Liberty and Packard) and [Agricole Farm Stop](https://www.agricolefarmstop.com/) in Chelsea

**Restaurants:** Their produce is served at Zingerman's Roadhouse, Spencer, and Sava's in Ann Arbor.
