Washtenaw Meats raises grass-fed and pasture-raised animals on their Dexter-area farm and sells directly to consumers through bulk meat shares and individual cuts available online.

## Meat Share Options

- **Beef:** Quarter, half, and whole animal shares — grass-fed and finished
- **Pork:** Half and whole pig — pasture-raised
- **Lamb:** Whole and half lamb — pasture-raised
- **Poultry:** Whole chickens and other poultry cuts

Shares are sold by estimated hanging weight. Contact for current pricing and scheduling.

## How It Works

Order online through their website, receive a quote based on available animals, pay a deposit, then pick up your finished meat at the farm in Dexter when ready.

## Why Buy Direct

Direct-from-farm meat shares typically cost less per pound than grocery store prices and guarantee you know exactly how your animal was raised.
