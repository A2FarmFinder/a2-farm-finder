Wild Apple Farms is a small, diversified farm in Dexter growing vegetables and raising hens using organic practices. The farm offers CSA subscriptions combining produce, eggs, and honey for a complete weekly share.

## CSA Options

- **Produce + Eggs:** $70 for 18 weeks
- **Honey add-on:** $150/season
- **Seasonal bundles** also available

Shares are picked up at the farm or at designated drop points. Contact the farm for 2026 signup and availability.

## Farm Practices

Wild Apple Farms does not use synthetic pesticides, herbicides, or fertilizers. Animals are pasture-raised.
