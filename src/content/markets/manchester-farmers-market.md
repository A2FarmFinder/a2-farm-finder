The Manchester Farmers Market runs Thursday afternoons in the heart of downtown Manchester, bringing together local growers and food producers from the surrounding area.

## Market Details

- **Address:** Adrian St & Main St, Manchester, MI 48158
- **Hours:** Thursdays, 3:00pm – 6:00pm
- **Season:** May 15 – October 16

## What to Expect

Local produce, cheese, eggs, honey, baked goods, and a variety of goods from vendors within 100 miles of Manchester.

## Payment

Accepts cash, SNAP/EBT, Senior Market Bucks, Manchester Community Resource Center tokens, and FMR coupons.

**Contact:** farmersmarket48158@gmail.com
