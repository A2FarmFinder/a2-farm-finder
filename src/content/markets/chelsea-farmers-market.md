The Chelsea Farmers Market runs twice a week from May through October:
Saturdays 8am-1pm at Palmer Commons (304 S Main St) and Wednesdays 10am-3pm
at Chelsea State Bank (1010 S Main St). Look for Tantre Farm and Community
Farm of Ann Arbor among the regular vendors, plus a children's activity tent
and live music throughout the season.
