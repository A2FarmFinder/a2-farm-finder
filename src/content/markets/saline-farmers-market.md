The Saline Farmers Market runs Saturdays all year. From May 2 through
October 31 it's outdoors at Lot #4 (100 S Ann Arbor St), 8am-12pm. From
November through April it moves indoors to Liberty School (7265 Ann
Arbor-Saline Rd), 9am-1pm. Expect produce, meat, fish, poultry, cheese, eggs,
baked goods, crafts, and free entertainment in summer.
