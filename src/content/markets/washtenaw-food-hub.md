The Washtenaw Food Hub is a nonprofit organization founded in 2011 with a mission to support local food producers and improve community access to local food. At the heart of the hub is the **Sunflower Farm Market** — a public, year-round market open to everyone with no membership required.

## Sunflower Farm Market

**Address:** 4175 Whitmore Lake Rd, Ann Arbor, MI 48105

**Hours:**
- Wednesday & Friday: 4pm – 8pm
- Saturday & Sunday: 9am – 5pm

**What's available:**
- Fresh seasonal produce sourced from local farms
- In-house prepared foods — ready-to-eat meals and sides made by their chefs
- Artisan breads, locally made cheese, and locally roasted coffee
- Handcrafted goods from local artisans

## About the Washtenaw Food Hub

Beyond the market, the Food Hub provides commercially licensed processing kitchens and storage rental space for local food entrepreneurs, hosts events and educational programs, and runs ecological demonstration projects. It serves as infrastructure for the local food economy — supporting small farmers and food businesses while connecting the community with local food year-round.

Visit [thewashtenawfoodhub.com](https://www.thewashtenawfoodhub.com) for current market updates and event announcements.
