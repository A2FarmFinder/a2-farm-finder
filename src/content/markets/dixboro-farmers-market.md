The Dixboro Farmers Market brings local farmers and artisans to the Dixboro Village Green every Friday afternoon from late May through October.

## Market Details

- **Address:** Dixboro Village Green, 5221 Church St, Ann Arbor, MI 48105
- **Hours:** Fridays, 3:00pm – 6:00pm
- **Season:** May 23 – October 24

## What to Expect

Fresh local produce, baked goods, farm eggs, flowers, and handcrafted items from local vendors.

## Payment

Accepts cash, SNAP/Bridge/EBT, and Double Up Food Bucks.
