The Trinity Health Farm Stand brings fresh, affordable produce directly to the Saint Joseph Mercy Hospital campus in Ypsilanti every Wednesday. Part of Trinity Health's mission to connect the community with healthy, local food.

## Market Details

**Two locations on the same day:**
- **Morning:** 5301 McAuley Dr, Ypsilanti — 11:00am – 1:00pm
- **Afternoon:** 5557 McAuley Dr, Ypsilanti — 2:00pm – 6:00pm
- **Season:** April 8 – November 18

## What to Expect

Fresh local produce and vegetables sourced from local farms. The stand is designed to make healthy food accessible to hospital employees, patients, and community members.

## Payment

Accepts cash, credit/debit, SNAP/EBT, and Prescription for Health program vouchers.
