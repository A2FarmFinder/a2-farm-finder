The Westside Farmers' Market is a beloved neighborhood market on Ann Arbor's west side, hosted in the Zingerman's Roadhouse parking lot on Thursday evenings from mid-July through late September.

## Market Details

- **Address:** 2501 Jackson Ave, Ann Arbor, MI 48103 (Zingerman's Roadhouse lot)
- **Hours:** Thursdays, 3:00pm – 7:00pm
- **Season:** July 17 – September 25

## What to Expect

Fresh local produce, pasture-raised meats, baked goods, farm eggs, honey, and handcrafted goods from local vendors.

## Payment

Accepts cash and most vendors accept credit cards.
