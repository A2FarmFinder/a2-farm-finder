White Lotus Farms is an Ann Arbor farm operation offering fresh, locally grown products at two convenient locations.

**Farm Cart — 7217 W Liberty Rd, Ann Arbor**
Open Wednesdays 9am–1pm and Saturdays 9am–2pm, year-round. The cart moves inside during the coldest winter months. This is the farm's original location.

**Café & Market — 355 S Zeeb Rd, Suite C, Ann Arbor**
Open Tuesday through Saturday, 7am–7pm. Closed Sunday and Monday. This newer location combines a café with a farm market selling White Lotus products.

**What they sell:** White Lotus Farms specializes in microgreens, artisan and aged cheeses, organic baked goods, and botanical skincare products made from farm-grown ingredients.

They also maintain vendor booths at the Ann Arbor Farmers Market (Wednesday and Saturday, May–December) and Eastern Market in Detroit (Saturdays).

Check their website or Instagram [@whitelotusfarms](https://www.whitelotusfarms.com/) for the most current hours, as seasonal schedules can vary.
