The Dexter Winter Farmers Market fills the gap when most outdoor markets close for the season. Held indoors at the Dexter Wellness Center, it runs every 1st and 3rd Saturday from November through April, giving the community year-round access to local food.

## Market Details

- **Address:** 7643 Huron River Dr, Dexter, MI 48130 (Dexter Wellness Center)
- **Hours:** 1st & 3rd Saturdays, 9:00am – 1:00pm
- **Season:** November – April

## What to Expect

Local meats, poultry, eggs, cheese, root vegetables, preserved foods, honey, syrup, baked goods, and handcrafted items from area vendors who continue farming and producing through the winter.

## Payment

Accepts cash; many vendors accept cards.
