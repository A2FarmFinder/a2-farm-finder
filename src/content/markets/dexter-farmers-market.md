The Dexter Farmers Market is held at a covered downtown location at 3233 Alpine
St and runs twice a week during the season: Saturdays 8am–1pm from May through
October, and Tuesdays 11am–3pm from June through September.

Celebrating its 20th year in 2026, the market features fresh local food, produce,
and crafts from area vendors. The market accepts SNAP/Bridge cards, PEBT, and
participates in Double Up Food Bucks (matching up to $10 daily for fresh produce),
as well as Senior Market Bucks.
