The Ypsilanti Farmers Market is run by [Growing Hope](https://growinghope.net/), a Ypsilanti-based nonprofit dedicated to building a just and equitable local food system through food access, urban agriculture, and community programs.

**Saturday Market:** May 16 – October 31, 9am–1pm at 16 S Washington St (Black Lives Matter Blvd), Ypsilanti.

**Tuesday Evening Market:** June 2 – August 25, 3–7pm at the same location. The evening market is a great option for those who can't make it on Saturday mornings.

**Mini Markets at B-Cubed Bakery:** Select dates throughout the year (May 2, May 9, July 4, November 7) at 741 Emerick St, 9am–1pm.

The market features a mix of local farmers, specialty food producers, and artisan vendors. As a Growing Hope initiative, the market prioritizes equity and food access — SNAP/EBT is accepted.
