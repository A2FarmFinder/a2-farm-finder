The Whitmore Lake Farmers Market is hosted by Northfield Township on Sunday mornings in downtown Whitmore Lake, bringing together local growers and makers from mid-June through late September.

## Market Details

- **Address:** 9725 Main St, Whitmore Lake, MI 48189
- **Hours:** Sundays, 10:00am – 2:00pm
- **Season:** June 1 – September 28

## What to Expect

Fresh local produce, baked goods, homemade goods, and artisan products from area vendors.
