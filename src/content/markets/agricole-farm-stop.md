Agricole Farm Stop is Chelsea's destination for locally sourced food, carrying products from more than 50 Michigan farms and food producers. Part farm store, part café, it's open seven days a week year-round in the heart of downtown Chelsea.

## Store Details

- **Address:** 118 N Main St, Chelsea, MI 48118
- **Hours:** Mon–Fri 7:00am – 7:00pm | Sat–Sun 8:00am – 6:00pm
- **Open:** Year-round

## What to Find

Fresh local and seasonal produce, pasture-raised and grass-fed meats, artisan cheese and dairy, farm eggs, freshly baked bread and pastries, prepared foods, local honey, specialty grocery items, and coffee drinks — all sourced from 50+ Michigan farms and producers.

## Why Shop Here

Agricole does the sourcing work for you: every product on the shelf comes from a named local farm. Great for stocking up on CSA-style variety without a subscription commitment.
