Located in Kerrytown, the Ann Arbor Farmers Market has connected local
producers with shoppers for over 90 years. It runs year-round: Wednesdays
and Saturdays (7am-3pm) from May through December, with additional Wednesday
evening hours (4-8pm) June through October, and Saturday-only (8am-3pm) from
January through April.
