The Pittsfield Township Farmers Market is held every Thursday afternoon at the township's Administration Building campus, at the corner of Platt Rd and West Michigan Ave.

## Market Details

- **Address:** 6201 W Michigan Ave, Ann Arbor, MI 48108 (Platt Rd & Michigan Ave)
- **Hours:** Thursdays, 2:00pm – 6:00pm
- **Season:** May 7 – October 8
- **Rain or shine**

## What to Expect

Fresh local and seasonal produce, plants, flowers, farm eggs, pasture-raised meats, baked goods, honey, pasta, and artisan goods. The market also features gardening advice, chef demos and tastings, live music, and weekly kids crafts and activities.

## Payment

Accepts cash, credit/debit cards (via vendors), SNAP/EBT, Double Up Food Bucks, and Prescription for Health programs.

**Transit:** AAATA Flex Ride service available.
