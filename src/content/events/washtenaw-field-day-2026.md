Washtenaw Field Day brings together regenerative row crop farmers and technical experts from across Michigan and the Midwest for a day of hands-on learning at Maulbetsch Farm.

The day includes in-field demonstrations, simulation stations, and informational tables covering regenerative practices. Soil scientists will be on hand to talk through soil sampling techniques, and there's dedicated time built in for peer networking with other area farmers.

**Cost:** Free, but registration is required — the agenda and registration link are posted on the [event page](https://washtenawcd.org/farmers/washtenaw-field-day).

Coffee, pastries, and lunch are provided at no extra charge. Hosted by the Washtenaw County Conservation District.
