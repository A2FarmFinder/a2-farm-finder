One of Michigan's longest-running Independence Day parades, the Ypsilanti 4th of July Parade has been a community tradition for 96 years. It's a great family event with a fantastic route through some of Ypsilanti's most iconic landmarks.

## Parade Details

- **Date:** Saturday, July 4, 2026
- **Time:** 11am – 12:30pm
- **Start:** Ypsilanti Water Tower, Cross St, Ypsilanti
- **Route:** Along Cross St to Historic Depot Town
- **End:** Rice Street, Historic Depot Town
- **Admission:** Free — watch from anywhere along the route

## Tips

- The Ypsilanti Water Tower area and Depot Town both offer great viewing spots
- Depot Town has restaurants and shops open before and after the parade — make a morning of it
- No registration needed, just show up

For more information visit [ypsi4th.com](https://www.ypsi4th.com) or follow the Ypsilanti 4th of July Parade on Facebook.
