One of the most popular farm festivals in the Ann Arbor area, the Lavender Bloom Festival at Jacob's Fresh Farm draws thousands of visitors each July to pick lavender, explore artisan vendors, and enjoy a full day on the farm.

## Event Details

- **Dates:** Saturday, July 18 – Sunday, July 19, 2026
- **Hours:** 10am – 5pm both days
- **Location:** Jacob's Fresh Farm, 475 N Fletcher Rd, Dexter, MI 48130
- **Admission:** $5 per person — children under 12 free

## What's There

- **Lavender picking** — over 2,000 lavender plants in bloom
- **Raspberry picking**
- **50+ artisan vendors**
- **5 food trucks**
- **Free bounce house**
- **Free arts and crafts**
- **Free entertainment**
- **Face painting**
- **Toddler play area**

## Tips

- Arrive early — this event draws large crowds, especially on Saturday
- Wear comfortable shoes for walking the fields
- Bring cash for vendors (cards also accepted at most)
- Check Jacob's Fresh Farm on Facebook for the most current updates before you go

[Jacob's Fresh Farm](https://jacobsfreshfarm.com) is a certified organic u-pick farm in Dexter, just a short drive from Ann Arbor.
