The Milan Farm & Flea Fall Market is the second of two annual market events at Wilson Park in Milan. Fall is a great time to visit — harvest season means an abundance of late-summer and fall produce, and the cooler weather makes for a comfortable day outdoors.

## Event Details

- **Date:** Saturday, September 27, 2026
- **Hours:** 9am – 4pm
- **Location:** Wilson Park, 89 Wabash Rd, Milan, MI 48160
- **Admission:** Free and open to the public

## What to Expect

**50+ vendors** selling farm-fresh produce, local honey, locally raised meats, handmade crafts, jewelry, antiques, plants, and more. Live music and food trucks on site throughout the day.

Wilson Park's amenities — playground, disc golf, and walking trails — are open during the event.
