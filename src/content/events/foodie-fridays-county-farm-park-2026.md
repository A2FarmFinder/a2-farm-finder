County Farm Park hosts local food trucks every Friday through the growing season, near its farm-themed playground and community gardens.

## Details

- **Dates:** Every Friday, May 15 – September 25, 2026
- **Time:** 11:00am – 7:00pm (or until trucks sell out)
- **Location:** County Farm Park, Platt Rd entrance, 2230 Platt Rd, Ann Arbor, MI 48104
- **Admission:** Free

Truck lineup varies week to week — check the park's listing for current vendors.
