This expanded field day at Maulbetsch Farm brings together regenerative farming practitioners, technical experts, and farmers from across Michigan and the Midwest for a hands-on day of learning in the field.

## What to Expect

- **In-field demonstrations** of regenerative row crop practices
- **Soil health testing and simulation stations**
- **Expert speakers** — reputable Michigan and Midwest-based regenerative practitioners
- **Peer networking** with other farmers and agricultural professionals
- **Informational tables** from supporting organizations

## Who It's For

This event is geared toward farmers, agricultural professionals, and serious enthusiasts interested in regenerative and sustainable farming practices. It's a working field day — expect to be outside on a farm for most of the day.

## Location

Maulbetsch Farm, 2271 E Northfield Church Rd, Ann Arbor, MI.

## More Information

Organized by the [Washtenaw County Conservation District](https://washtenawcd.org/). Check their events page for registration details and updates as the event approaches.
