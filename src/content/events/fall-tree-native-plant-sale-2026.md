The Washtenaw County Conservation District's annual Fall Tree & Native Plant Sale is a great opportunity to pick up native trees and plants at reasonable prices while supporting local conservation efforts.

**Pre-orders** open August 3rd — ordering ahead guarantees your selection. Visit [washtenawcd.org](https://washtenawcd.org/) to pre-order when the time comes.

**Walk-in:** Extra plants are available during the final hour (5–6pm) on a first-come, first-served basis without a pre-order.

## Why Native Plants?

Native trees and plants support local pollinators, birds, and wildlife, require less water and maintenance once established, and are well-suited to Michigan's soil and climate. A great companion to a backyard vegetable garden or a farmers market habit.

## Location

Washtenaw Farm Council Grounds, 5055 Ann Arbor-Saline Rd, Ann Arbor, MI 48103.

Organized by the [Washtenaw County Conservation District](https://washtenawcd.org/).
