The Washtenaw County Conservation District hosts a community potluck lunch at Loonfeather Park, bringing together rain garden volunteers, Conservation District supporters, and alumni of the Michigan Naturalist and Conservation Stewards (SNAG) programs.

## Details

- **Date:** Saturday, July 11, 2026
- **Time:** 11:30am – 1:30pm
- **Location:** Loonfeather Park, 1320 Grove Rd, Ypsilanti, MI 48198
- **Admission:** Free

Bring a dish to pass. A guided tour of the park's rain garden follows lunch.
