The Ann Arbor Civic Band kicks off the 4th of July holiday week with a free outdoor concert at Burns Park. The concert is themed "With Liberty & Justice for All" — patriotic music performed live under the summer sky.

## Details

- **Date:** Wednesday, July 1, 2026
- **Time:** 7:30pm
- **Location:** Burns Park, 1300 Baldwin Ave, Ann Arbor, MI 48104
- **Admission:** Free

Bring a lawn chair or blanket. All ages welcome. No tickets needed — just show up.

The Ann Arbor Civic Band performs a free summer concert series at Burns Park on Wednesday evenings throughout the summer. This is their Independence Day themed performance.
