The Saline Community Fair is a week-long celebration of the area's agricultural roots, held annually at the Washtenaw Farm Council Grounds just outside Saline. Expect livestock shows, tractor pulls, and a full slate of family entertainment spread across five days.

Highlights for 2026 include a Car and Tractor Show, a Pony Pull, a Super Kicker Rodeo, and the Super Stock and Modified Tractor Pull, alongside live music from the Saline Fiddlers Philharmonic and the RFD Boys.

Full schedule and ticket information is available at [salinefair.org](https://www.salinefair.org/). Admission pricing was not posted at the time of writing — check the site closer to the fair for gate prices.
