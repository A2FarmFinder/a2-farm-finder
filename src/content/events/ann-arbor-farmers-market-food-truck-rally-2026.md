The Ann Arbor Farmers Market hosts a Food Truck Rally on the third Wednesday of each month from July through October, right after the regular Wednesday Market wraps up. Local food trucks, food carts, and pop-ups set up alongside live music and other activities.

## Details

- **Date:** 3rd Wednesday of the month, July–October 2026 (next: Wednesday, July 15, 2026)
- **Time:** 5:00pm – 8:00pm
- **Location:** Ann Arbor Farmers Market, 315 Detroit St, Ann Arbor, MI 48104
- **Admission:** Free

Check the [Ann Arbor Farmers Market page](https://www.a2gov.org/parks-and-recreation/parks-and-places/ann-arbor-farmers-market/) for the specific truck lineup each month.
